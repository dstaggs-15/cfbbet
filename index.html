College Football Pick'em Tracker

  1. Weekly games: edit data/games.txt (format: Team A vs. Team B (+spread, O/U)) and push. The Action rebuilds public/games.json.
  2. Make picks: click a team. You’ll see a gold highlight + “Selected: Team” badge. Enter a fake bet (e.g., 10) and hit Lock.
  3. Settle results: enter the two final scores and hit Settle. We grade ATS (picked team score + spread vs opponent).
  4. Stats: right panel shows W-L-P, net profit, profit chart, and history.
  5. Offline + backups: works offline; data lives in your browser. Use Export/Import for backups.
  6. Note: v1 pays even money (win +bet / loss −bet / push 0). We can change to −110 vig on request.
